Reading Log!

Here is a list of books I've read recently and my review on them!

Breaking Dawn by Stephenie Meyer

Finished: July 14, 2025

I'm happy that Bella and Edward finally got their happy ending. I'm sure the Volturi will probably be more annoying in the coming books though. 7.5/10 sounds like it was written for a teenager by a teenager.

Obsidio by Amie Kaufman and Jay Kristoff

Finished: July 10, 2025

AHHHHHHH!!!! I love this book and the book series so much. I've already gushed soo much about this book in my tribute to the series but I have to say, I love the little details Kaufman and Kristoff add to the story this is what makes it great!! This is probably the 7th or 8th time I've reread it. 11/10 book

Children Of Blood and Bone by Tomi Adeyemi

Finished: July 20, 2025

Pretty riveting book. I think Zelie and Inan deserved a better ending, in the following books you can really see how much he loves her. 9/10 book, the cliffhanger was so mean

Children of Virtue and Vengeance by Tomi Adeyemi

Finished: July 10, 2025

I liked this book better than the first one in the series. Amari betraying Zelie after Zelie taught her how to use her magic properly was not it though. 9.5/10 book

Dry by Neal Shusterman

Finished: June 28, 2025

A gripping, dystopian tale that kept me on the edge of my seat. I picked this book up in the morning and did not put it down until I finished it late at night. I love Neal Shusterman's writing, the way he uses words so effectively to convey a point or a scene. Also hits close to home because I live in California, so it reminded me about how cooked we are!
